# /idea — Generate Content Ideas

AI-powered content ideation from vault knowledge, trending topics, and audience interests.

## Arguments
- `/idea` — Generate 10 ideas
- `/idea --viral` — Only high-viral-potential ideas
- `/idea "niche"` — Ideas for specific sub-niche

## Steps

1. **Mine vault** for unexplored topics (51K+ notes)
2. **Cross-reference** with trending searches
3. **Apply** viral scoring:
   - Search volume (is anyone looking for this?)
   - Competition (are others covering it?)
   - Uniqueness (can only Paul make this?)
   - Timeliness (is this relevant now?)
4. **Output** ranked list of 10 ideas with scores
