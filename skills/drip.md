# /drip — Drip Content Publisher

Publish content on a timed drip schedule — space out posts for maximum algorithmic engagement.

## Arguments
- `/drip queue "content"` — Add to drip queue
- `/drip status` — Show queue
- `/drip run` — Publish next item

## Steps

1. **Manage drip queue** in `~/.mirrordna/bus/drip_queue.json`
2. **Optimal spacing**: 4-6 hours between posts on same platform
3. **Cross-platform stagger**: YouTube → 2h → Bluesky → 2h → Mastodon → 2h → dev.to
