# /behind-scenes — Behind-the-Scenes Content

Generate BTS content showing the real human story behind the code.

## Arguments
- `/behind-scenes` — Auto-generate from today's session

## Steps

1. **Extract** the human moments:
   - What broke and how you fixed it
   - Decisions made and why
   - The emotional arc of the session
2. **Generate** Bluesky thread in first person
3. **Create** a "real talk" Short clip concept
4. **Write** beacon reflection post
