# /growth — Growth Metrics Dashboard

Pull growth metrics from all platforms and generate trend analysis.

## Steps

1. **YouTube**: Subs, views, watch hours, CTR, AVD
2. **Bluesky**: Followers, engagement rate
3. **Mastodon**: Followers, boosts
4. **GitHub**: Stars, forks, profile views
5. **Website**: Pageviews (if analytics enabled)
6. **Beacon**: Post views, RSS subscribers
7. **Generate** trend chart (text-based) and recommendations
