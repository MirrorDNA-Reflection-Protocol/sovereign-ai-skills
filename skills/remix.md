# /remix — Remix Old Content

Take older videos/posts and remix them with new footage, updated data, and fresh SEO.

## Arguments
- `/remix <video_id>` — Remix specific video

## Steps

1. **Analyze** original video performance
2. **Identify** what's changed since original
3. **Generate** updated script
4. **Create** "updated" version with new footage
5. **Optimize** title: "X — Updated for 2026"
