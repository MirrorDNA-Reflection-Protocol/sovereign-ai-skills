# /cta — Generate Call-to-Action Variants

Generate and test different CTAs for videos, posts, and pages.

## Arguments
- `/cta video` — End-of-video CTAs
- `/cta social` — Social post CTAs
- `/cta site` — Website CTAs

## Steps

1. **Generate 10 CTA variants** via Ollama using frameworks:
   - Direct: "Subscribe for daily sovereign AI builds"
   - Value: "Join 100+ developers building without the cloud"
   - Curiosity: "Tomorrow I'm shipping something that changes everything"
   - Social proof: "This community is building the future of personal AI"
2. **A/B test** in upcoming content
