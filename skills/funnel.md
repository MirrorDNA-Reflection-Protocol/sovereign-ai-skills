# /funnel — Content Funnel Analysis

Map the viewer journey from discovery to subscriber to community member.

## Steps

1. **Map funnel stages**:
   - Discovery: Shorts, search, suggested videos
   - Engagement: Watch time, likes, comments
   - Subscription: Sub rate per video type
   - Community: Discord/community post engagement
   - Advocacy: Shares, referrals
2. **Identify leaks** (where viewers drop off)
3. **Generate** 5 fixes for biggest leak
