# /launch — Content Launch Sequence

Execute a coordinated multi-platform launch for a major piece of content.

## Arguments
- `/launch <video_id>` — Launch sequence for video
- `/launch --paper "title"` — Launch sequence for paper

## Steps

1. **T-1h**: Teaser post on Bluesky
2. **T-0**: Publish video/paper
3. **T+5m**: SEO optimize
4. **T+15m**: Bluesky thread (10 posts)
5. **T+30m**: Mastodon post
6. **T+1h**: dev.to article
7. **T+2h**: Beacon blog post
8. **T+4h**: Quote cards on Bluesky
9. **T+24h**: Follow-up engagement post
10. **T+48h**: Share metrics + thank community
