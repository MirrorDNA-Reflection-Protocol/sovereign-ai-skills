# /collab — Find Collaboration Opportunities

Identify potential collaboration targets: YouTubers, open source projects, communities in the sovereign AI niche.

## Steps

1. **Search** for channels/creators in adjacent niches (1K-100K subs)
2. **Analyze** content overlap and audience fit
3. **Draft** outreach messages (personalized, value-first)
4. **Output** ranked list of 10 collaboration targets with contact info
