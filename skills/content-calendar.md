# /content-calendar — Content Calendar Management

View, plan, and manage the content calendar for all platforms.

## Arguments
- `/content-calendar` — Show this week
- `/content-calendar plan` — Generate next week's plan
- `/content-calendar --month` — Monthly view

## Steps

1. **Show scheduled content**:
   - LaunchAgent schedules (daily-video, auto-publish, weekly-video)
   - Upcoming planned videos/posts
2. **Identify gaps** in the schedule
3. **Generate** content ideas for empty slots
4. **Output** as formatted calendar
