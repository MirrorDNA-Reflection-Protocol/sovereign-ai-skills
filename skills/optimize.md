# /optimize — Full Content Optimization Sweep

Run a comprehensive optimization pass on all YouTube videos and social profiles.

## Steps

1. **Audit** all YouTube videos (titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails)
2. **Run** youtube_seo.py --recent on last 20 videos
3. **Check** channel keywords, about section, links
4. **Verify** all social bio links point to activemirror.ai
5. **Generate** improvement report
